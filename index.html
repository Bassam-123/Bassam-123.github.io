Projects

Mitigation of Backdoor Poisoning Attacks in Deep Neural Networks

Implemented a backdoor attack mitigation system using DBSCAN clustering to identify potential poisoned data samples containing patch triggers in the CIFAR-10 dataset. Applied heatmaps to visualize the spatial distribution of these malicious points, efficiently localizing trigger positions; implemented inpainting techniques to fill the trigger. Reduced the overall attack accuracy by 90% by re-training the model.

Activity Volume Predictions in Twitter

Developed multiple regression models, including linear regression, ridge regression, and lasso regression, for predicting the number of active discussions on Twitter. Analyzed using different metrics like R² Score, Mean Absolute Error, Root Mean Square Error, and cross-validation scores. Observed that lasso regression is the best model as its generalization was better than other models, achieving an R² Score of 0.92.

Seoul Bike Rental Prediction

Conducted a comprehensive data regression analysis to develop a predictive model for Seoul Bike Rentals by utilizing 12 months of historical data on bike rentals, weather conditions, and timestamps to understand usage patterns. Applied XGBRegressor with 1500 gradient-boosted trees and a 0.01 learning rate. Created a model predicting the required number of bikes for rental with an R² Score of 0.91.
